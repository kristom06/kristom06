CHARLOTTE

Charlotte Linlin, better known as Big Mom, is the captain of the Big Mom Pirates and was formerly one of the Four Emperors ruling over the New World, as the only female member of that group. She is also the matriarch of the very large Charlotte Family, whose many children make up the infrastructure of her crew.

JEWERLY BONNEY

Jewelry Bonney, also known as the "Big Eater" is a pirate and the captain of the Bonney Pirates. She and her crew originated from South Blue. She is one of twelve pirates who are referred to as the Worst Generation, and prior to the two-year timeskip had a bounty of 140,000,000

VINSMOKE REIJU

Vinsmoke Reiju, also known as "Poison Pink", is the eldest child and only daughter of the Vinsmoke Family. She is a princess of the Germa Kingdom and a commander in its military arm, Germa 66.
